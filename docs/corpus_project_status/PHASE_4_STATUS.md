## Usage Scenarios

### Scenario 1: New Customer Service Rep Starts
1. Read welcome_pack.md (understand customer perspective)
2. Complete compliance_training_manual.md (mandatory)
3. Complete product_training_manual.md (learn products)
4. Study call_centre_script.md (practice scripts)
5. Review email_templates.md and sms_templates.md (understand communications)
6. Ready to take calls within 1 week

### Scenario 2: New Branch Employee Starts
1. Read product_training_manual.md (learn products)
2. Complete compliance_training_manual.md (mandatory)
3. Complete branch_operations_training.md (daily procedures)
4. Shadow experienced teller (2 weeks)
5. Ready to handle transactions

### Scenario 3: Customer Has Complaint
1. Customer reads complaint_resolution_guide.md (understands process)
2. Staff member uses sop_exception_handling.md + call_centre_script.md to handle
3. Email templates used for communication (email_templates.md)
4. SMS alerts sent using sms_templates.md
5. Follow-up documented in complaint_resolution_guide.md timeline

### Scenario 4: Annual Compliance Update
1. All staff complete compliance_training_manual.md (renewed)
2. Staff sign acknowledgment
3. Certificate issued
4. Training recorded in HR system
5. Compliance verified in annual audit
