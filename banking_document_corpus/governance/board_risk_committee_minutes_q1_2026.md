### 3. Capital Adequacy & Stress Testing

**Summary:**
CFO presented capital position and 2026 stress testing scenarios.

**Current Capital Position:**
- Capital Ratio: 18.2% (Pillar 1: 15% minimum)
- Capital Buffer: 3.2% above minimum
- Assessment: **Comfortable position**, no capital raising needed 2026

**Stress Testing Scenarios (2026 Plan):**
1. **Recession Scenario:** 2% economic contraction
   - Expected capital impact: -2.1% (ratio falls to 16.1%)
   - Assessment: **Resilient**, still above minimum
   
2. **Fraud Shock:** £250k fraud incident
   - Expected capital impact: -0.3% (ratio falls to 17.9%)
   - Assessment: **Manageable**, standard reserves sufficient
   
3. **Operational Loss:** Major system outage, customer losses
   - Expected capital impact: -0.8% (ratio falls to 17.4%)
   - Assessment: **Absorbed**, recovery plan documented

**Committee Decision:**
✅ **APPROVED** 2026 stress testing plan.

**Action Items:**
- CFO to conduct stress tests by end of Q2 2026
- Report findings to committee in June 2026 meeting

### 7. Technology Risk Assessment

**Summary:**
CTO presented technology risk assessment including cybersecurity posture and system availability.

**Key Risks Identified:**

**Risk 1: Legacy Payment System** ⚠️ **Medium Risk**
- System is 7 years old, periodic slowness
- Mitigation: Replacement project planned 2027
- Status: Monitoring and optimization
- Impact if failure: 1–2 hour payment processing delay

**Risk 2: Third-Party Vendor Reliance** 🟢 **Low Risk**
- Dependent on AWS for cloud infrastructure
- Mitigation: Multi-region backup, vendor SLAs
- Status: Quarterly vendor reviews ongoing
- Impact: Well-mitigated

**Risk 3: Cybersecurity Threat Landscape** 🟡 **Emerging**
- Increased phishing attacks (industry-wide)
- Mitigation: Staff training, advanced email filtering
- Status: Monitoring external threat intelligence
- Impact: Low (strong controls in place)

**Committee Decision:**
✅ **APPROVED** technology risk assessment.

**Recommendation:**
- Accelerate legacy payment system replacement (tentatively 2027 → early 2027)
- Continue cybersecurity investment and awareness training
