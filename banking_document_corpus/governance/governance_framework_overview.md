## Purpose

Establish FirstUK Bank's governance framework, defining Board responsibilities, executive authority, internal controls, and regulatory compliance structure.

---

## Governance Principles

FirstUK Bank operates according to the following governance principles:

1. **Board Leadership:** Board provides strategic direction and risk oversight
2. **Clear Authority:** Defined responsibilities at each level (Board, Executive, Managers)
3. **Independent Challenge:** Risk and Audit committees provide independent assurance
4. **Transparency:** Clear communication with regulators and stakeholders
5. **Accountability:** Individuals held responsible for their decisions
6. **Compliance:** Full compliance with FCA, PRA, and legal requirements
7. **Ethics:** High ethical standards in decision-making and operations

---

## Board of Directors

### Composition

- **Number of Directors:** 5–7 (per FCA guidance)
- **Chair:** Independent Non-Executive Director
- **CEO:** Executive director (sole executive on Board)
- **Independent NEDs:** Majority of board
- **Expertise:** Mix of banking, risk, finance, compliance expertise

### Board Responsibilities

**Strategic:**
- Approve business strategy
- Approve annual budget and capital plan
- Approve material acquisitions/disposals
- Set risk appetite

**Governance:**
- Approve policies and procedures
- Approve committee charters
- Monitor compliance
- Appoint executives

**Risk & Control:**
- Oversee risk management
- Review internal controls
- Receive audit reports
- Monitor capital adequacy

**Stakeholder Relations:**
- Regulatory engagement
- Shareholder communication
- Public disclosures
- Crisis management

### Board Meetings

- **Frequency:** Quarterly (minimum)
- **Attendance:** All directors expected
- **Agenda:** Strategic, governance, risk, financial matters
- **Papers:** Provided 1 week in advance
- **Minutes:** Approved and retained (permanent record)

## Executive Management

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

**Authority:**
- Chief executive responsibility
- Business performance
- Regulatory interface
- Crisis management

**Reports to:** Board

**Key Reports:**
- Monthly: Financial performance
- Quarterly: Risk dashboard
- Annually: Strategy review

---

### Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

**Authority:**
- Financial management and reporting
- Treasury and funding
- Capital management
- Investment decisions

**Reports to:** CEO and Board

---

### Chief Risk Officer (CRO)

**Authority:**
- Enterprise risk management
- Capital and liquidity management
- Risk policy approval
- Regulatory compliance oversight

**Reports to:** Board Risk Committee (functionally), CEO (administratively)

**Independence:** Separate from operational departments

---

### Chief Operating Officer (COO)

**Authority:**
- Business operations
- Process management
- Operational efficiency
- Customer service

**Reports to:** CEO

---

### Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

**Authority:**
- Technology strategy and infrastructure
- System availability and security
- Technology investment decisions
- Cybersecurity

**Reports to:** CEO

---

### Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

**Authority:**
- Information security policy and procedures
- Cybersecurity incident response
- Data protection and privacy
- Security risk assessment

**Reports to:** CTO (functionally), Board Risk Committee (for security risk)

---

## Internal Controls Framework

### Three Lines of Defense

**Line 1: Business Units**
- Responsible for their own controls
- Day-to-day risk management
- Daily monitoring and escalation

**Line 2: Risk & Compliance**
- Independent risk oversight
- Compliance testing
- Control design review
- Key control monitoring

**Line 3: Internal Audit**
- Independent assurance
- Annual audit plan (risk-based)
- Finding identification and follow-up
- Reports to Board Audit Committee

## Regulatory Framework

### Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

**Regulatory Handbook:**
- COBS (Conduct of Business)
- SYSC (Systems and Controls)
- DISP (Dispute Resolution)
- SUP (Supervision)

**Compliance Requirements:**
- Customer identification (KYC)
- Anti-money laundering (AML)
- Fair treatment and complaints
- Market conduct
- Financial crime prevention

---

### Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA)

**Rulebook Areas:**
- Capital adequacy (Pillar 1)
- Risk management (Pillar 2)
- Operational resilience (Pillar 2)
- Liquidity management

**Compliance Requirements:**
- Minimum capital ratios
- Risk governance
- System availability (RTO/RPO)
- Stress testing

---

## Compliance Monitoring

### Regulatory Engagement

- **Supervisory Contact:** Designated FCA/PRA supervisor
- **Quarterly Meetings:** With supervisory team
- **Annual Reporting:** Compliance data to FCA
- **Self-Disclosure:** Voluntary breach reporting

### Internal Monitoring

- **Monthly:** Compliance dashboard (key metrics)
- **Quarterly:** Risk committee review
- **Semi-Annual:** Control testing (audit)
- **Annual:** Full governance review

### Audit & Assurance

- **Internal Audit:** Annual audit plan, quarterly reporting
- **External Audit:** Annual financial audit
- **Regulatory Audit:** FCA/PRA periodic examinations

---

## Ethics & Conflicts of Interest

### Conflict of Interest Policy

- Declaration required (all staff/directors)
- Regular review (annual minimum)
- Escalation procedure (if conflict arises)
- Restriction on transactions (where appropriate)

### Ethics Standards

- All decisions made in customer's best interest
- No conflicts override customer interests
- Transparency in communications
- Fair dealing with stakeholders

---

## Reporting & Transparency

### External Reporting

- **Annual Report:** Financial position, strategy, governance
- **Regulatory Reports:** Compliance data, capital ratios
- **Regulatory Circulars:** Updates on regulatory changes
- **Customer Communications:** Fair, transparent, timely

### Internal Reporting

- **Board Reports:** Quarterly (financial, risk, governance)
- **Risk Dashboard:** Monthly (operational, compliance, fraud)
- **Audit Reports:** Quarterly (findings, follow-up)
- **Staff Communications:** Regular (policies, updates, training)
