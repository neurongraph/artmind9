# GENERAL QUESTIONS

## About FirstUK Bank

**Q: What is FirstUK Bank?**  
A: FirstUK Bank is a UK retail bank offering savings accounts, current accounts, and mortgages. We're committed to competitive rates, transparent pricing, and excellent customer service.

**Q: Where is FirstUK Bank based?**  
A: We're headquartered in London with 12 branches across the UK (London, Manchester, Leeds, Birmingham, Bristol, Edinburgh, Glasgow, Liverpool, Norwich, Southampton, and Belfast).

**Q: Is my money safe with FirstUK Bank?**  
A: Yes. We're regulated by the FCA and PRA. Your deposits are protected by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) up to £85,000.

**Q: How do I contact FirstUK Bank?**  
A: 
- **Phone:** 0800-555-2265 (Mon–Fri 08:00–20:00, Sat 09:00–17:00)
- **Email:** support@firstuk.bank
- **Branch:** Visit any of our 12 locations
- **Online Chat:** Available at www.firstuk.bank (business hours)

**Q: Does FirstUK Bank offer business banking?**  
A: Currently, we focus on retail banking. Business accounts are on our roadmap for 2027.

# SMARTSAVER ACCOUNT

**Q: What is the SmartSaver Account?**  
A: Our flagship savings account with competitive interest rates (4.50%–4.80%), no monthly fees, and easy access to your money anytime.

**Q: What interest rate will I earn?**  
A: Rates depend on your balance:
- £0–£10,000: 4.50% AER
- £10,001–£50,000: 4.70% AER
- £50,001+: 4.80% AER

**Q: Is the interest rate fixed or variable?**  
A: Variable. Your rate adjusts monthly with the Bank of England base rate. We maintain a fixed 2.5% margin above BoE, so if BoE changes, your rate changes proportionally.

**Q: When is interest paid?**  
A: Monthly, on the last business day of each month. Interest is calculated daily.

**Q: What are the fees?**  
A: No monthly fees. Only optional fees:
- International ATM withdrawal: £2.50
- International transfer: £5.00
- Card replacement: £15.00

**Q: Can I withdraw my money anytime?**  
A: Yes, anytime with no penalties or waiting periods. Easy access is a core feature.

**Q: What's the minimum opening balance?**  
A: Just £1. No minimum to open, no minimum to keep the account open.

**Q: Can I open a joint account?**  
A: Yes. Two customers can hold one SmartSaver Account together. Both have equal rights to withdraw.

**Q: How do I open an account?**  
A: Three ways:
1. **Online:** www.firstuk.bank/open-account (1–2 days)
2. **In Branch:** Visit any FirstUK location (same day)
3. **By Post:** Call 0800-555-2265 to request pack (5–7 days)

**Q: What documents do I need?**  
A: 
- Photo ID (UK Passport or Driving License)
- Proof of address (utility bill, council tax, or bank statement)

# CURRENT ACCOUNT

**Q: What is the Current Account?**  
A: Our checking account for everyday spending, with no monthly fees, standing orders, and optional overdraft facility.

**Q: What are the fees?**  
A: No monthly fee. Overdraft interest (if used) at 15% AER for arranged overdraft.

**Q: Can I set up standing orders?**  
A: Yes. Set up recurring payments to anyone, anytime.

**Q: Can I use Direct Debits?**  
A: Yes. Authorize billers to take payments automatically, with full protection under Direct Debit Guarantee.

**Q: Is there an overdraft?**  
A: Optional. You can arrange an overdraft limit of £500–£5,000 if eligible.

---

# MORTGAGES

**Q: Does FirstUK Bank offer mortgages?**  
A: Yes. We offer residential mortgages with fixed rates (2 and 5-year terms) or variable rates.

**Q: What are current rates?**  
A: 
- Fixed 2-year: 4.50%–5.25% (depends on LTV)
- Fixed 5-year: 5.00%–5.75% (depends on LTV)
- Variable: BoE Base + 1.50%–2.25%

**Q: How much can I borrow?**  
A: Up to £500,000 for residential property.

**Q: How long is the loan term?**  
A: 2–25 years (you choose).

**Q: What's the application process?**  
A: 
1. Request mortgage information
2. Submit application + documents
3. Property valuation
4. Underwriting review (5–10 days)
5. Offer issued
6. Legal completion
7. Funds released

**Q: How long does the process take?**  
A: Typically 4–6 weeks from application to completion.

# ONLINE & MOBILE BANKING

**Q: Is online banking secure?**  
A: Yes. We use bank-level security (TLS 1.3 encryption, two-factor authentication, 15-minute session timeout).

**Q: Do you have a mobile app?**  
A: Yes. Download "FirstUK Bank" from Apple App Store or Google Play Store (iOS 13+, Android 9+).

**Q: Can I use biometric login?**  
A: Yes. Mobile app supports fingerprint and face ID for quick, secure access.

**Q: Can I transfer money from home banking?**  
A: Yes. Online banking and mobile app both support money transfers to other banks (1–2 business days).

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Completely. We comply with GDPR and never share your data with third parties for marketing.

# OPENING AN ACCOUNT

**Q: What's the quickest way to open an account?**  
A: Online at www.firstuk.bank/open-account (1–2 business days).

**Q: Can I open an account in a branch?**  
A: Yes. Visit any FirstUK branch and staff will help you (usually same day or next day).

**Q: Can I open an account by post?**  
A: Yes. Call 0800-555-2265 to request the postal application pack (5–7 business days).

**Q: What identification do I need?**  
A: UK Passport or Driving License (original or certified copy).

**Q: What address proof do I need?**  
A: Utility bill, council tax letter, or bank statement (dated within 3 months).

**Q: How old do I need to be?**  
A: 18 years old (minimum age for retail banking accounts).

**Q: What if I can't provide these documents?**  
A: Contact us at support@firstuk.bank — we may be able to help with alternative verification.

# FEES & CHARGES

**Q: Does FirstUK Bank charge hidden fees?**  
A: No. All fees are clearly shown in our Pricing Guide at www.firstuk.bank/pricing

**Q: What are the main fees?**  
A:
- **Monthly Account Fee:** £0 (all products)
- **International ATM:** £2.50 per withdrawal
- **International Transfer:** £5.00
- **Card Replacement:** £15.00 (expedited: £25.00)

**Q: Are there early repayment penalties (mortgages)?**  
A: Yes, for fixed-rate mortgages (1–3% of outstanding balance). Variable rates have no early repayment fee.

**Q: Will my fees change?**  
A: We may change fees with 30 days notice. You can close your account with no penalty if you disagree.

A: We want to fix it. Contact us:
- **Phone:** 0800-555-2265
- **Email:** support@firstuk.bank
- **Branch:** Visit any location

# SECURITY & FRAUD

**Q: What if my card is lost or stolen?**  
A: Call 0800-555-2265 immediately. We'll freeze the card and send a replacement (3–5 days, or overnight for £25).

**Q: What if I'm a victim of fraud?**  
A: Report immediately (0800-555-FRAUD). We'll investigate and refund you within 10 days if unauthorized.

**Q: How do I protect my account?**  
A:
- Never share your PIN or password
- Use safe ATMs only
- Monitor transactions regularly
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Use biometric login on mobile app

**Q: Is my data protected?**  
A: Yes. FSCS protects up to £85,000 per customer per bank.

# ABOUT RATES & INTEREST

**Q: Why do interest rates change?**  
A: Bank of England (BoE) changes its base rate based on economic conditions. We pass these changes to customers (our 2.5% margin stays fixed).

**Q: When does FirstUK change rates?**  
A: Monthly (typically mid-month). We notify customers via email before changes take effect.

**Q: Can I lock in a rate?**  
A: SmartSaver is variable. For fixed rates, consider fixed-rate bonds (coming 2026).

**Q: How is interest calculated?**  
A: Daily calculation, paid monthly. Example: £10,000 at 4.50% AER earns ~£37.50 per month.
