# SmartSaver Account FAQ

## Account Basics

**Q: What is the SmartSaver Account?**  
A: It's an easy-access savings account that earns variable interest. You can deposit and withdraw money anytime with no penalties or waiting periods. It's designed for customers who want a flexible savings account with competitive interest rates.

**Q: Who can open a SmartSaver Account?**  
A: Anyone who is:
- At least 18 years old
- A UK resident
- Able to provide UK Passport or Driving License
- Able to provide proof of address

**Q: Is there a minimum balance requirement?**  
A: No. You can open an account with just £1.00. There's no minimum balance to keep the account open.

**Q: How do I open a SmartSaver Account?**  
A: You can open an account:
- **Online:** Visit www.firstuk.bank and click "Open Account"
- **In Branch:** Visit any FirstUK Bank branch
- **By Post:** Call 0800-555-2265 to request postal application pack

**Q: How long does it take to open an account?**  
A: 
- Online/Branch: 1–2 business days
- Postal: 5–7 business days

## Interest Rates & Earnings

**Q: What interest rate do I earn?**  
A: It depends on your balance:
- £0–£10,000: 4.50% AER
- £10,001–£50,000: 4.70% AER
- £50,001+: 4.80% AER

**Q: Is the interest rate fixed or variable?**  
A: Variable. The rate changes monthly based on the Bank of England base rate. If the BoE raises rates, your rate rises. If BoE cuts rates, your rate falls.

**Q: How is interest calculated and paid?**  
A: 
- **Calculation:** Daily (interest accrues every day)
- **Payment:** Monthly (paid on the last business day of the month)
- Example: £10,000 at 4.50% earns about £37.50 per month

**Q: Can my interest rate decrease?**  
A: Yes. If the Bank of England cuts its base rate, your rate will decrease (but our margin of 2.5% remains constant).

**Q: When will my rate next change?**  
A: Rates are reviewed monthly (typically mid-month). If there's a change, it becomes effective on the 1st of the following month. We'll email you if your rate changes.

**Q: What if I don't like the new interest rate?**  
A: You can:
- Keep your account (accept new rate)
- Switch to SmartSaver Plus (if balance >£50k)
- Close your account (no penalties)
- Switch to a current account or other product

## Deposits & Withdrawals

**Q: How do I deposit money?**  
A: You can deposit via:
- **Bank Transfer:** FPS (immediate), BACS (next day)
- **Branch Deposit:** Cash (at any FirstUK branch)
- **Direct Transfer:** From salary/pension (set up with employer)

**Q: How do I withdraw money?**  
A: You can withdraw via:
- **ATM:** Withdraw cash (£500/day limit)
- **Bank Transfer:** Send to another bank account (1–2 days)
- **Branch Teller:** Withdraw cash or check

**Q: Are there withdrawal limits?**  
A: No limits on transfers to other accounts. ATM withdrawals limited to £500/day.

**Q: Is there a charge to withdraw money?**  
A: 
- UK ATM withdrawal: £0
- International ATM withdrawal: £2.50
- Transfer to another account: £0

**Q: How quickly will my withdrawal go through?**  
A:
- ATM cash withdrawal: Immediate
- Transfer to another bank: 1–2 business days
- Teller withdrawal: Immediate (if cash available)

## Fees & Charges

**Q: Are there monthly fees?**  
A: No. There's no monthly maintenance fee for SmartSaver Account.

**Q: What fees might I be charged?**  
A: Only these optional fees:
- International ATM withdrawal: £2.50 per withdrawal
- International transfer: £5.00 per transfer
- Card replacement: £15.00 (or £25 for expedited)

**Q: Is there an overdraft fee if I go negative?**  
A: SmartSaver doesn't offer overdraft. If you try to withdraw more than your balance, the transaction is declined (no fee, but no withdrawal).

**Q: What is the debit card cost?**  
A: 
- Initial card: £0
- Replacement card: £15 (or £25 for overnight delivery)

## Standing Orders & Direct Debits

**Q: Can I set up a standing order from my SmartSaver Account?**  
A: Yes. You can set up regular, fixed transfers to another account:
- Frequency: Weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually
- Amount: Any amount
- Payee: Any person or business (provide their account details)

**Q: How do I set up a standing order?**  
A: Online (easiest):
1. Log into online banking
2. Click "Standing Orders"
3. Add payee and details
4. Confirm amount and frequency
5. Standing order active

Or visit branch for staff assistance.

**Q: Can I cancel a standing order?**  
A: Yes, anytime with no notice period. Cancellation effective immediately. No fees for cancellation.

**Q: What is a Direct Debit?**  
A: Authorization for a company (like utility company) to withdraw money from your account. Different from standing order (they initiate withdrawals, not you).

**Q: How do I set up a Direct Debit?**  
A: When paying a company, request to "set up Direct Debit." They'll provide a mandate form. You'll receive confirmation when active.

**Q: Am I protected if there's unauthorized Direct Debit?**  
A: Yes. Direct Debit Guarantee:
- ✅ Refund within 10 business days
- ✅ No questions asked
- ✅ Full refund amount

## Joint Accounts

**Q: Can I open a joint SmartSaver Account?**  
A: Yes. Two customers can hold one joint account.

**Q: Who can access the money in a joint account?**  
A: Either account holder can withdraw the full balance anytime. Both have equal rights.

**Q: What happens to a joint account if one person dies?**  
A: Surviving account holder retains the account. Estate of deceased person entitled to their share (typically 50%). Bank requires death certificate and legal documentation.

**Q: What if joint account holders split up?**  
A: Both retain legal rights to the full account balance. Either can freeze/close the account. We recommend legal advice (civil matter, not bank's decision).

## Fraud & Security

**Q: What if someone uses my card fraudulently?**  
A: 
- Report immediately: 0800-555-FRAUD
- We investigate
- **If unauthorized:** Full refund within 10 days (no liability)
- **If you were negligent** (shared PIN): We may share liability

**Q: How do I protect my account?**  
A:
- ✅ Never share PIN or password
- ✅ Use safe ATM locations
- ✅ Monitor transactions regularly
- ✅ Report suspicious activity immediately
- ✅ Use biometric login (if available) on mobile app

**Q: What if I lose my debit card?**  
A: Call 0800-555-2265 immediately to freeze the card. We'll send a replacement (£15) within 3–5 business days, or overnight (£25).

## Account Management

**Q: How do I check my balance?**  
A: 
- **Online Banking:** Log in to www.firstuk.bank
- **Mobile App:** Open FirstUK Bank app
- **Phone:** Call 0800-555-2265
- **ATM:** Insert card and check balance
- **Branch:** Ask teller

**Q: How do I access online banking?**  
A: 
1. Visit www.firstuk.bank/login
2. Enter username and password
3. Complete two-factor authentication (OTP via email/SMS)
4. Access your account

**Q: Can I close my account anytime?**  
A: Yes. No penalties or notice required. To close:
- Call 0800-555-2265
- Visit a branch
- Submit online form

Your final balance will be transferred (check, bank transfer, or cash).

**Q: What happens if my account becomes dormant?**  
A: If no activity for 12 months:
- Account marked "dormant" (no impact on you)
- Interest still earned
- You can still deposit/withdraw
- We'll send a letter to confirm status

## SmartSaver Plus

**Q: What is SmartSaver Plus?**  
A: Premium tier of SmartSaver for larger balances (£50,001+). Earns 5.25% interest (vs. standard tier at 4.80%).

**Q: Who qualifies for SmartSaver Plus?**  
A: Account holders with balance over £50,001.

**Q: What are the extra benefits?**  
A:
- Higher interest rate (5.25% vs. 4.80%)
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority customer service
- No international transfer fees

**Q: How do I upgrade to Plus?**  
A: Automatic when your balance exceeds £50,001. No application needed.

**Q: What if my balance drops below £50,001?**  
A: You'll drop back to standard SmartSaver rates (4.80% for Tier 3). Can upgrade again anytime your balance exceeds £50,001.

## General Questions

**Q: Is my money protected if FirstUK Bank fails?**  
A: Yes. Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) protects up to £85,000 per customer.

**Q: What is the debit card limit for contactless payments?**  
A: £100 per transaction. No PIN required below this limit.

**Q: Can I get interest statements?**  
A: Yes. Email support@firstuk.bank to request statement of interest earned.

**Q: How do I update my contact details?**  
A: 
- Online: Update in account settings
- Phone: Call 0800-555-2265
- Branch: Visit in person

**Q: Can I change the account holder details after opening?**  
A: No. Account holder cannot be changed. You can close and open a new account in different name.
