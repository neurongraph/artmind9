## Branch Operations

### Daily Operations

**Opening Checklist:**
- Cash vault verification
- ATM stocking and balance
- System connectivity test
- Security alarm test
- Staff briefing

**See:** Daily Operations Checklist (DOC-001)

**Closing Checklist:**
- Cash reconciliation
- ATM cash count
- System shutdown
- Security alarm activation
- End-of-day report

### Cash Handling

**Policy:** Cash Handling Procedures (CHP-001)

- **Daily Cash Limit:** £20,000 per branch
- **Vault Security:** Dual custody (manager + officer)
- **Reconciliation:** Daily EOD
- **Dispute Resolution:** <£1,000 threshold for branch manager approval

**Armored Transport:** Provided by external contractor (Brinks)  
**Collection Schedule:** 3x weekly (Mon, Wed, Fri)

### Security

**Branch Security Measures:**
- CCTV (24/7 recording, 30-day retention)
- Alarm system (monitored by external provider)
- Safe/vault (dual custody)
- Employee ID badges
- Customer ID verification for account access

**See:** Branch Security Policy (SEC-POL-001)

### Opening Hours

**Standard Hours:**
- **Monday–Friday:** 09:00–17:00
- **Saturday:** 09:00–13:00
- **Sunday/Public Holidays:** Closed

**Extended Hours (London Head Office):**
- **Monday–Friday:** 08:00–18:00 (staff only, customer hours 09:00–17:00)

### Customer Facilities

**Typical Branch Layout:**
- Teller counter (cash services)
- Account opening desk
- Waiting area with seating
- ATM (external)
- Customer restroom
- Meeting room (private interviews)

## Staffing Model

### Branch Manager (1 per branch)

**Responsibilities:**
- Branch operations oversight
- Customer service escalation
- Complaint management
- Cash handling supervision
- Staff scheduling
- Local marketing

**Qualifications:** Banking experience, customer service, compliance knowledge  
**Reporting to:** Director of Retail Banking

### Branch Officer (0–1 per branch)

**Responsibilities:**
- Customer service (counter, phone)
- Account opening support
- Cash handling
- Customer inquiries
- Branch administration

**Qualifications:** Banking/customer service, cash handling training  
**Reporting to:** Branch Manager

### Head Office Staff (3 at Head Office)

## Branch Compliance

### Regulatory Compliance

**Key Compliance Areas:**
- KYC/CDD (see [[business_ontology]], KYC-SOP-001)
- AML screening (AML-POL-002)
- Complaint handling (COM-POL-006)
- Customer privacy (GDPR, PRI-POL-004)
- Data handling (DPA)

**Compliance Certification:** All branch managers certified annually  
**Audit Schedule:** Annual audit of branch controls

**See:** Compliance Bulletins (COMP-BULL-2026)

### Document Retention

**Policy:** Document Retention Policy (RET-POL-005)

- **Customer Records:** 6 years post-account closure
- **Transaction Records:** 6 years
- **Complaint Records:** 6 years
- **AML Records:** 6 years

### Data Security

- **Customer Data:** Encrypted, access-controlled, audit-logged
