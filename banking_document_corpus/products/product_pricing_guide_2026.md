# SMARTSAVER ACCOUNT (SAV-001)

## Interest Rates

**Effective:** 2026-01-15  
**Rate Type:** Variable (reviewed monthly)  
**Base Calculation:** Bank of England Base Rate + 2.5% Margin

### Interest Rate Tiers

| Balance Tier | Annual Interest Rate (AER) | Annual Interest Rate (Gross) |
|---|---|---|
| £0–£10,000 | 4.50% | 4.50% |
| £10,001–£50,000 | 4.70% | 4.70% |
| £50,001+ | 4.80% | 4.80% |

**Interest Accrual:** Monthly (calculated daily, paid on last business day of month)

**Example:** £10,000 balance in Tier 1
- Monthly interest: £37.50 (= £10,000 × 4.50% ÷ 12)
- Annual interest: £450.00

### Variation Basis

- **Review Frequency:** Monthly
- **Trigger:** Bank of England Base Rate changes
- **Basis:** BoE Base Rate + 2.5% Margin (fixed margin)
- **Notification:** Email alert when rate changes (customer can opt-in)
- **Effective Date:** Rate change effective 1st of following month

### Current BoE Base Rate: 4.0% (as of 2026-01-15)

## Interest Rates

**Rate Options:**
- Fixed Rate 2 Year
- Fixed Rate 5 Year
- Variable Rate (tracker)

### Current Mortgage Rates (2026-01-15)

| Product | Loan-to-Value (LTV) | Fixed 2-Yr | Fixed 5-Yr | Variable |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Residential Mortgage | <60% LTV | 4.50% | 5.00% | Base + 1.5% |
| Residential Mortgage | 60–80% LTV | 4.75% | 5.25% | Base + 1.75% |
| Residential Mortgage | 80%+ LTV | 5.25% | 5.75% | Base + 2.25% |

**Fixed Rate 2-Year:** Fixed for 2 years, then re-fixed

**Early Repayment Fee (Fixed Rate):** Typically 1–3% of outstanding balance (if early repayment during fixed period)
