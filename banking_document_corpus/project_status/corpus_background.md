
## Baseline Scope for artmind Demonstration

## Vision

Create a realistic, fictional UK retail bank (**FirstUK Bank**) that serves as a reusable enterprise knowledge corpus for demonstrating artmind's capabilities in:

- Knowledge Lakehouse
- Knowledge Graph construction
- GraphRAG
- Hybrid Search
- Enterprise Ontologies
- Agentic AI
- Enterprise Knowledge Management

The objective is to model an enterprise rather than create isolated sample documents.

---

# Objectives

Demonstrate how enterprise knowledge emerges from the combination of:

- Business documents
- Operational data
- Enterprise metadata
- Business ontologies
- Document ontologies
- Concept ontologies
- Process models
