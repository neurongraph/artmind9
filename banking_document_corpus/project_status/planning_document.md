# Deliverables
Create synthetic enterprise knowledge corpus markdown documents for the following:
## Phase 1 — Enterprise Design

- Organisation model
- Products
- Departments
- Branches
- Systems
- Business ontology

## Phase 2 — Knowledge Corpus

30–40 interconnected enterprise documents.

## Phase 3 — Operational Dataset

Synthetic operational data with realistic relationships.

## Phase 4 — Knowledge Assets

- Document schemas
- Extraction schemas
- Ontologies
- Graph mappings
- Metadata models

## Phase 5 — Demonstrations

Curated scenarios showcasing:

- Enterprise search
- GraphRAG
- Hybrid retrieval
- Agentic reasoning
- Policy compliance
- Operational intelligence

---

# Guiding Principle

Design the corpus as a **digital twin of an enterprise**, where every document, process, policy, operational event, and business entity belongs to a coherent organisational model. The resulting corpus should be reusable across artmind demonstrations, Knowledge Lakehouse architecture discussions, ontology design, GraphRAG experiments, and Agentic AI workflows.
