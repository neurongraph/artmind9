## Knowledge Management Best Practices

### Organization
- Documents organized by phase (enterprise → governance → operations → support)
- Clear metadata identifying ownership and status
- Consistent naming conventions

### Maintenance
- Version control via metadata
- Related document links for cross-referencing
- Regular update procedures (e.g., annual compliance renewal)
- Change tracking (Document Version History tables)

### Governance
- Clear document ownership (Head of X)
- Approval and sign-off procedures
- Review dates and expiration
- Escalation chains for changes

### Accessibility
- Plain English explanations
- Step-by-step procedures
- Practical examples and scenarios
- Quick reference guides (checklists, tables)
