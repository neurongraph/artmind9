```

Bank interest rate has been updated.

- Previous Rate: [old rate]%
- New Rate: [new rate]%
- Effective Date: [date]

Your SmartSaver rate is variable, which means it moves with the

BoE, so you're always competitive.

What This Means:
Your monthly interest will change starting next month. If rates 
increased, you'll earn more. If they decreased, you'll earn less.

Example Calculation:

FirstUK Bank Team
```

## Guidelines for All Templates

**Tone:** Friendly, professional, clear, reassuring  
**Personalization:** Always use customer's name  
**Clarity:** Explain what happened in plain English  
**Action Items:** Be clear about next steps  
**Contact Info:** Always include support phone/email  
**Brand:** Use FirstUK Bank logo and signature block
