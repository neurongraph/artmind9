## WHY RATES VARY

### SmartSaver Rates (Variable)

**Our rates change because:**
1. **Bank of England decisions:** BoE raises/lowers base rate
2. **Market conditions:** Deposit rates in market change
3. **FirstUK strategy:** Competitive positioning
4. **Cost of funds:** Deposit costs change with market

**Your benefit:** If market rates fall, you're not locked into old rate

**Your risk:** If rates rise, you keep new higher rate

---

### Mortgage Rates (Fixed vs. Variable)

**Fixed Rate Benefit:**
- ✅ Rate guaranteed for 2–5 years
- ✅ Monthly payment predictable
- ✅ Protected if BoE raises rates

**Fixed Rate Risk:**
- ⚠️ Early repayment penalties if you refinance
- ⚠️ Rate re-fixes at end of term (may be higher)

**Variable Rate Benefit:**
- ✅ Lower initial rate (usually)
- ✅ No early repayment penalties
- ✅ Benefit if BoE cuts rates

**Variable Rate Risk:**
- ⚠️ Monthly payment unpredictable
- ⚠️ Payment rises if BoE raises rates

## FACTORS AFFECTING YOUR INTEREST

### What INCREASES Your Interest Earned

✅ **Increase Balance:** Move from Tier 1 to Tier 2/3 (higher rate)  
✅ **BoE Rate Rise:** Automatic rate rise (2.5% margin maintained)  
✅ **Switch to Plus:** SmartSaver Plus earns 5.25% if £50k+ balance  

### What DECREASES Your Interest Earned

❌ **Decrease Balance:** Drop below £10,000 threshold  
❌ **BoE Rate Cut:** Automatic rate cut (margin fixed, so decline together)  
❌ **Market Competition:** If competitors raise rates, FirstUK may follow suit (but typically match)

## FEES THAT REDUCE NET INTEREST

### International ATM Withdrawal

```
Scenario: Withdraw £500 abroad

Gross Interest Earned (SmartSaver): £450/year (4.50%)
Less: International ATM fees: £2.50 per withdrawal

If you withdraw £500 x 1 time/month:
  Monthly ATM fees: £2.50 × 12 = £30/year
  Net interest after fees: £450 - £30 = £420/year
  Effective rate: 4.20% (vs. stated 4.50%)
```

**Tip:** Use domestic ATMs only (no fee) to keep full interest rate

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Why is my interest rate lower than my friend's?**  
A: Your interest rate depends on your balance tier. If your balance is below £10,000, you earn Tier 1 rate (4.50%). Your friend earning 4.70% likely has £10,001+ in their account.

**Q: Will my rate ever go down?**  
A: Yes. If Bank of England cuts its base rate, your rate will decline (we maintain 2.5% margin over BoE).

**Q: Can I lock in today's rate?**  
A: SmartSaver is a variable rate product. Rates adjust monthly with market changes. If you want a fixed rate, consider a fixed-rate bond (future product).

**Q: When is interest paid?**  
A: Last business day of each month. Interest calculated daily and paid as lump sum each month.
