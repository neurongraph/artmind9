## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When will I earn interest?**  
A: Your first interest payment will be at the end of the month following your opening. After that, monthly on the last business day.

**Q: Can I withdraw my money anytime?**  
A: Yes, completely. No waiting periods, no penalties. SmartSaver is fully accessible.

**Q: How do I change my rate?**  
A: You can't (it's variable), but you'll always be competitive. We guarantee a fixed 2.5% margin above the Bank of England rate.

**Q: Is my money safe?**  
A: Completely. We're regulated by the FCA and PRA, and your deposits are protected by FSCS (up to £85,000).

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Log in to online banking, click "Forgot Password," and follow the link sent to your email.

**Q: Can I set up standing orders?**  
A: Yes, once your account is open, log in and select "Payments" to set up recurring transfers.

**Q: What if I lose my card?**  
A: Call 0800-555-2265 immediately. We'll block it and send a replacement (3–5 days free, or overnight for £25).

**Q: Can I open a joint account?**  
A: Yes! Both account holders have equal access and withdrawal rights. Contact us for details.

## Important Terms (In Plain English)

**AER** = Annual Equivalent Rate (tells you how much interest you earn per year)

**BACS** = Standard bank transfer (takes 1–3 days)

**FPS** = Faster Payments (takes minutes to hours)

**Direct Debit** = Authorization for a company to auto-deduct from your account (protected)

**Escalation** = When your issue needs a manager or higher authority to resolve
