## PART 1: SmartSaver Account

### Product Overview

**What It Is:**  
A fully accessible savings account with variable interest rates tied to the Bank of England base rate.

**Who It's For:**
- Customers who want to save money
- Anyone looking for competitive interest rates
- People who may need to access their money quickly
- Both individuals and joint account holders

**Key Benefit:**
Earn competitive interest (4.50%–4.80% AER) while maintaining full access to your money.

### How to Describe It to Customers

**Simple Explanation:**
"SmartSaver is a savings account where your money earns interest every month. You can withdraw anytime with no penalties, and our rates are among the best available."

**Detailed Explanation:**
"You deposit money in your SmartSaver account. Every month, we calculate interest based on your balance and the current rate, then add it directly to your account. Your rate moves with the Bank of England base rate, so it adjusts automatically each month. There are no monthly fees, no withdrawal restrictions, and no minimum balance."

### Features & Benefits

| Feature | What It Means | Customer Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Variable Rate** | Moves with BoE | You benefit when rates rise |
| **4.50%–4.80% AER** | Tiered by balance | Highest rate for higher balances |
| **Monthly Interest** | Paid every month | Compounding — interest earns interest |
| **No Fees** | £0/month | Keep more of your money |
| **Full Access** | Withdraw anytime | Emergency access if needed |
| **No Minimums** | Open with £1 | Easy for anyone to start |
| **Joint Accounts** | Two people per account | Great for couples or family |

### Interest Rate Mechanics

**The Formula:**
BoE Base Rate + 2.5% (FirstUK margin) = Your Rate

**Example:**
- BoE is 4.0%
- FirstUK adds 2.5%
- Your rate = 6.5% AER

**If BoE Changes:**
- BoE rises to 4.5% → Your rate rises to 7.0%
- BoE falls to 3.5% → Your rate falls to 6.0%

**Why This Is Good:**
Your 2.5% margin never changes. You're always getting a competitive rate without FirstUK cutting margin.

### Tier Structure

| Balance Range | AER Rate |
|---|---|
| £0–£10,000 | 4.50% |
| £10,001–£50,000 | 4.70% |
| £50,001+ | 4.80% |

**Customer Question:** "What if I deposit more — does my rate go up immediately?"  
**Answer:** "Yes, once your balance moves into the higher tier, your new rate applies the next month."

### Interest Calculation

**Example:**
- Balance: £10,000
- Rate: 4.50% AER
- Days in month: 30
- Daily rate: 4.50% ÷ 365 = 0.01233%
- Interest: £10,000 × 0.01233% × 30 = £36.99

**Monthly Interest Payment:**
- Calculated daily based on balance
- Paid on the last business day of month
- Added directly to account
- Doesn't require action from customer

### Competitors & Positioning

**How We Compare:**
- Chase: 4.35% (lower)
- Chip Bank: 4.40% (lower)
- FirstUK: 4.50%–4.80% (highest)
- Santander: 4.50% (equal)

**Our Advantage:** Wider tiered rates (up to 4.80%), no minimum balance, and reliable 2.5% margin.

### Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can my interest rate go down?**  
A: Yes, if the Bank of England base rate falls. Your rate always stays 2.5% above BoE.

**Q: Will my interest ever be locked in?**  
A: No, SmartSaver is always variable. If you want a fixed rate, ask about our Fixed Rate Bonds (coming 2026).

**Q: What if I withdraw money — do I lose interest?**  
A: No, you earn interest on your balance every day. If you withdraw on day 15, you earn interest for the first 15 days that month.

**Q: Can I have multiple SmartSaver accounts?**  
A: No, one account per customer. But you can have a SmartSaver AND a Current Account if you want.

**Q: Is there a notice period to access my money?**  
A: No, it's fully accessible. Withdraw anytime by online banking, ATM, or visiting a branch.

### Opening Process

**Steps:**
1. Customer visits website, branch, or calls
2. Chooses product (SmartSaver)
3. Provides ID and address proof (KYC)
4. AML screening
5. Risk assessment
6. Account created
7. Customer activates online login
8. First deposit made

**Timeline:**
- Online: 1–2 business days
- Branch: Same day or next day
- Postal: 5–7 business days

**What It Is:**  
A checking account for everyday spending with standing orders, Direct Debits, and optional overdraft.

**Who It's For:**
- Customers who need a primary bank account
- People who pay bills regularly
- Anyone who wants to combine savings + checking

**Key Benefit:**
All-in-one account for daily banking with no monthly fees.

### Features

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| **Debit Card** | Spend via card or contactless |
| **Standing Orders** | Set up recurring payments |
| **Direct Debits** | Authorize billers to auto-deduct |
| **Transfers** | Send money to any UK bank |
| **Overdraft** | Optional, up to £5,000 |
| **No Monthly Fee** | £0 to maintain |
| **Online Banking** | 24/7 access |

### Interest Rate

**Savings Rate:** 0% AER (no interest on balance)  
**Overdraft Rate:** 15% AER (if used)

### Opening Process

Same as SmartSaver (see above).

## PART 3: Mortgages

### Product Overview

**What It Is:**  
Loans for residential property purchase.

**Loan Terms:** 2–25 years  
**Maximum Loan:** £500,000  
**Rate Types:** Fixed (2/5-year) or Variable

### Fixed Rates

| Term | Rate Range |
|---|---|
| 2-Year | 4.50%–5.25% |
| 5-Year | 5.00%–5.75% |

**LTV Dependency:** Rates vary by Loan-to-Value ratio
- 80% LTV (20% deposit): Lowest rate
- 95% LTV (5% deposit): Highest rate

### Variable Rate

**BoE Base + Margin:** 1.50%–2.25% depending on LTV

### Customer Value Proposition

- Competitive fixed rates
- Clear rate structure by LTV
- Professional underwriting
- Transparent process
- Local branch support

## PART 5: How to Sell Products

### The Discovery Questions

Before recommending a product, ask:
1. **What's your main banking need?** (Savings? Checking? Borrowing?)
2. **Do you want to save or spend?**
3. **How often will you access the money?**
4. **Are you interested in earning interest?**
5. **Do you have other bank accounts?**

### Product Recommendations

**If Customer Says "I want to save money":**  
→ Recommend **SmartSaver**  
Pitch: "SmartSaver earns you 4.50%–4.80% AER with no fees and full access."

**If Customer Says "I need a main bank account":**  
→ Recommend **Current Account**  
Pitch: "Use your Current Account for everyday spending and bills, and your SmartSaver for savings."

**If Customer Says "I want to buy a home":**  
→ Recommend **Mortgage**  
Pitch: "Our fixed rates are competitive, and our process is straightforward."

### Handling Objections

**Objection: "Your rates sound high, are they really?"**  
**Response:** "Yes, they're among the best available. Compare us to Chase (4.35%), Santander (4.50%), and Chip (4.40%). We consistently rank in the top 3."

**Objection: "What if rates drop?"**  
**Response:** "Our rates will drop with the Bank of England, just like everyone's. But our 2.5% margin guarantees we stay competitive."

**Objection: "I'm happy with my current bank."**  
**Response:** "That's great. But consider opening a SmartSaver with us — you can keep your main account elsewhere and earn better interest here."

**Objection: "What if I need to close my account?"**  
**Response:** "No problem. You can close anytime with no penalty. We'll transfer your balance to your new bank."

## PART 6: Regulatory Context

### COBS (Conduct of Business Rules)

**Key Rules:**
- Explain products clearly
- Don't recommend unsuitable products
- Disclose all fees
- Obtain informed consent

**In Practice:**
- Always explain what each product is
- Ask about customer needs before recommending
- Show interest rates and fees clearly
- Get customer agreement before opening account

### GDPR & Data Protection

**Key Principles:**
- Only collect necessary data
- Use data only for stated purposes
- Protect data with encryption
- Honor customer data rights (access, deletion, portability)

**In Practice:**
- Don't ask for unnecessary information
- Explain how you'll use their data
- Secure all documents
- Respond to data requests within 30 days

### Know Your Customer (KYC)

**What We Need:**
- Photo ID (UK Passport or Driving License)
- Proof of address (utility bill, council tax, or bank statement)
- Source of funds (if relevant)
- Beneficial ownership (if account for company)

**Why It Matters:**
- Regulatory requirement
- Prevents fraud
- Protects customer's account
- Complies with AML regulations

## PART 7: Practical Scenarios

### Scenario 1: New Customer — Saving Money

**Customer:** "I've got £20,000 to put away. Where should it go?"

**Your Response:**
"Great! You're in our higher tier. With £20,000, you'd earn our premium rate of 4.70% AER on SmartSaver.

That's about £102/month in interest (£20,000 × 4.70% ÷ 365 × 30).

You can withdraw anytime if you need the money. No fees, no minimums. Would you like to open one?"

### Scenario 2: Existing Customer — Adding Product

**Customer:** "I have a Current Account here. Should I get your savings account too?"

**Your Response:**
"Absolutely! We recommend both. Use your Current Account for daily spending and bills, and open a SmartSaver for your savings.

Your Current Account earns 0%, but SmartSaver earns 4.50%–4.80% AER. It's a great way to maximize your interest without losing access to your money."

### Scenario 3: Rate Question

**Customer:** "Your 4.50% rate — is that guaranteed?"

**Your Response:**
"It's guaranteed not to drop below Bank of England + 2.5%. Right now BoE is 4.0%, so you get 6.5% AER. If BoE rises, so does your rate. If BoE falls, so does yours — but our 2.5% margin never changes.

This actually works in your favor if rates rise."
